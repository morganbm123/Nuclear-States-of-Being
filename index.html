Nuclear States of Being (WIP)
is a Master's thesis by Morgan Mueller developed
for the Interactive Telecommuncations Program
at New York University (2020).

    •   The nuclear fuel chain is a complex set of power dynamics that travel along a line of gnarled perceptibility, weaving in and out of lives, environments, and infrastructures at different points in time and space. The nuclear waste byproduct of this entangled nature has become its own site of controversy, financial gain, and history.

      This project synthesizes the complexity of the nuclear world's political ecologies through assemblage mapping techniques. It looks at knowledge gaps in the nuclear supply chain through the use of publically available records and satellite imagery. Finally this project looks at the current nuclear waste landscape and reimagines it as a design fiction in which the above ground nuclear waste cask is the semiotic container for the memorializtion of nuclear waste.
